Low Carb Crab Cakes


Ingredients

Crab Cakes
  • 8oz canned jumbo lump crab meat
  • 2TBL Red pepper, diced
  • 2TBL green pepper, diced
  • 1TBL light mayo
  • 1 egg, whisked
  • 6 Low carb crackers, crumbled
  • 1TBL Parsley, minced
  • 1TBLChive, minced
  • 1/2 Lemon, juiced
  • 1/2TBL Worcestershire
  • 1tsp Old bay
  • 1tsp Garlic powder
  • Pinch Salt
  • 1/4tsp pepper

Sauce
  • 1TBL light mayo
  • 1TBL Dijon
  • 1TBL lemon juiced
  • 1/4tsp swerve
  • 1/8tsp paprika



Instructions

  1. Gently combine, refrigerate 1 hr, strain then form into 4 balls.
  2. Placed on a lightly oiled baking sheet and bake 425Â° 10 min then under broiler for 3 min for an extra crispy crust
