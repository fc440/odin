Low Carb Crab Cakes



Ingredients

Crab Cakes
    8oz canned jumbo lump crab meat
    2TBL Red pepper, diced
    2TBL green pepper, diced
    1TBL light mayo
    1 egg, whisked
    6 Low carb crackers, crumbled
    1TBL Parsley, minced
    1TBLChive, minced
    1/2 Lemon, juiced
    1/2TBL Worcestershire
    1tsp Old bay
    1tsp Garlic powder
    Pinch Salt
    1/4tsp pepper

Sauce
    1TBL light mayo
    1TBL Dijon
    1TBL lemon juiced
    1/4tsp swerve
    1/8tsp paprika



Instructions

    Gently combine, refrigerate 1 hr, strain then form into 4 balls.
